# Client Onboarding Flow v1

## Purpose
This document defines how a new client becomes ready to use the platform.

The goal is to make the process:
- simple
- reassuring
- trust-focused
- fast enough to complete
- detailed enough to support safe bookings

---

## Core Onboarding Goal
A client should finish onboarding feeling:
- understood
- safe
- informed about platform boundaries
- ready to create a request or browse helpers

---

## Step 1 — Welcome / Role Confirmation
### Goal
Confirm that the user is entering as a client.

### Screen content
- headline: Get support around your appointment
- short explanation of what the platform does
- reminder that helpers provide non-medical support only
- CTA: Continue as Client

---

## Step 2 — Basic Profile Setup
### Required fields
- first name
- last name
- preferred language
- city
- district

### Optional fields
- profile image
- accessibility notes

### UX goal
Keep the first profile step light and easy.

---

## Step 3 — Emergency Contact
### Required fields
- emergency contact name
- emergency contact phone

### Why this matters
This adds trust and safety readiness for the booking experience.

### UX note
Explain clearly that this information is used for safety-related situations and is not shared unnecessarily.

---

## Step 4 — Support Preferences
### Optional or semi-structured fields
- preferred helper language
- preferred helper gender optional
- mobility notes
- support preferences
- anything that helps create safer bookings

### UX goal
Help clients personalize future requests without making this step feel heavy.

---

## Step 5 — Platform Boundaries and Trust
### Must show
- helpers provide non-medical support only
- the platform is not an emergency service
- helpers may not give medical advice or treatment
- in-app booking and payment should be used for safety

### Required acceptance
- terms
- privacy
- service boundaries

---

## Step 6 — First Action Prompt
After onboarding completion, client should be guided to one of two actions:
- Create a support request
- Browse helpers

### Preferred first CTA
Create your first request

### Secondary CTA
Browse trusted helpers

---

## Completion Condition
Client onboarding is complete when:
- required profile fields are completed
- emergency contact is completed
- required policy acknowledgements are accepted
