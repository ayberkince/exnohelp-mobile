# Startup Narrative v1
## What We Are
We are building a trust-first two-sided marketplace that connects people who need non-medical support before or after medical appointments with helpers who want flexible, meaningful side income.

## What Problem We Solve
There is a gap between formal healthcare and informal human support. People don't always need a nurse; sometimes they just need accompaniment, waiting support, or a calm presence. 

## Why This Product Is Different
It is not telemedicine, and it's not a generic gig app like TaskRabbit. It is narrowly focused on trust, clear boundaries, and marketplace coordination around vulnerable moments.
