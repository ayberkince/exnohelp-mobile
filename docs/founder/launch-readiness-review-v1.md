# Launch Readiness Review v1
## Purpose: Avoid premature launch or endless hesitation.
- **Product:** Can users onboard, book, and pay without major bugs?
- **Trust:** Can users report issues? Are boundaries clear?
- **Ops:** Can support handle verifications and refunds?
- **Infra:** Is production stable and monitored?
- **Marketplace:** Is there enough supply for the target segment?
