# Trust Signals v1

## Purpose
This document defines which trust markers are visible to users in the product.

Trust signals should help users make better decisions without implying false guarantees.

---

## Visible Trust Signals for Helpers
- profile photo
- verification badge
- languages
- completed bookings count
- average rating
- review count
- response rate
- completion rate
- service categories
- availability

---

## Visible Trust Signals for Clients
For v1, client trust visibility should be lighter, but some signals may include:
- completed profile
- booking history signal later
- identity readiness indicators where appropriate

---

## Trust Signal Rules
- signals must be earned through platform behavior or verification
- signals should never imply medical qualification unless formally part of a future regulated model
- signals should be understandable at a glance

---

## Important Note
Trust signals support decisions.
They do not remove the need for reporting, moderation, or platform safety policies.
