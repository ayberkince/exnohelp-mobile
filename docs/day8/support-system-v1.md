# Support System v1

## Purpose
This document defines how user support is handled on the platform.

The support system exists to resolve:
- booking confusion
- payment issues
- verification questions
- account review issues
- safety concerns
- general platform questions

---

## Support Entry Points
Users can reach support from:
- support center
- booking detail page
- trust and safety center
- review-state screen
- email support contact

---

## Support Categories
Recommended categories:
- booking help
- payment help
- refund question
- verification question
- trust and safety issue
- account restriction issue
- technical problem
- general question

---

## Support Prioritization
### High Priority
- safety concern
- serious booking issue in progress
- account review confusion around active booking
- financial error affecting live booking

### Medium Priority
- refund request
- repeated technical issue
- verification stuck

### Low Priority
- general product question
- profile help
- onboarding confusion

---

## v1 Support Principle
Support may be handled manually in v1.
This is acceptable if:
- requests are categorized
- important issues are prioritized
- outcomes are recorded
