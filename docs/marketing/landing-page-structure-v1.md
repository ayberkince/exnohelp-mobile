# Landing Page Structure v1

## Purpose
This document defines the homepage structure.

The landing page should convert 3 kinds of visitors:
- people who need support
- people who want to help and earn
- people evaluating trust before going further

---

## Homepage Sections

### 1. Hero Section
Must communicate:
- what the product is
- who it is for
- trust-first positioning
- two clear CTAs

### 2. How It Works
Simple 3-step explanation for clients and helpers.

### 3. For People Who Need Support
A section showing what kind of support can be booked.

### 4. For Helpers
A section showing how flexible side income works.

### 5. Trust and Safety
Public trust-building section:
- verification
- clear boundaries
- in-platform coordination
- non-medical only

### 6. Service Categories
Visual cards for:
- accompaniment
- waiting support
- return-home support
- paperwork support
- emotional companionship
- light practical help

### 7. Why People Use It
Short emotional + practical reasons.

### 8. FAQ
Address confusion, trust, and boundaries.

### 9. Final CTA Section
Strong final conversion section for both roles.

### 10. Footer
Links to:
- trust and safety
- FAQ
- terms
- privacy
- support
