# Brand Messaging v1

## Purpose
This document defines how the startup speaks publicly.

The brand must feel:
- calm
- trustworthy
- human
- clear
- respectful
- safe
- modern

It must not feel:
- clinical
- corporate-cold
- chaotic
- vague
- overly gig-economy aggressive
- medically misleading

---

## Core Brand Position
This is a trust-first platform for non-medical support around appointments and procedures.

It helps people book calm, practical human support when they need it, and helps helpers earn meaningful flexible income.

---

## Messaging Pillars

### 1. Human Support
This platform is about people helping people.

### 2. Clear Boundaries
Support is non-medical only.

### 3. Trust First
Verification, clear rules, and in-platform coordination matter.

### 4. Practical Simplicity
Booking support should be easy and understandable.

### 5. Calmness
The emotional tone should reduce stress, not increase it.

---

## Tone of Voice
The brand voice should be:
- warm
- direct
- reassuring
- simple
- respectful
- modern

Avoid:
- buzzwords
- exaggerated startup hype
- fake medical authority
- vague wellness language

---

## Public One-Sentence Version
Book trusted non-medical support around appointments and recovery moments.

## Public Longer Version
We connect people who need calm, practical support before or after medical appointments with verified helpers who provide non-medical assistance.
