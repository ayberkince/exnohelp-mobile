# FAQ v1

## 1. What kind of support can I book?
You can book non-medical support such as accompaniment to appointments, waiting support, return-home accompaniment, paperwork support, emotional companionship, and light practical help after an appointment.

## 2. Is this medical care?
No. This platform provides non-medical support only.

## 3. Can helpers give medical advice?
No. Helpers are not allowed to diagnose, treat, administer medication, or provide clinical care through the platform.

## 4. Is this an emergency service?
No. The platform is not an emergency service.

## 5. How do I know who I am booking?
Helper profiles include trust-oriented information such as profile details, service categories, languages, reviews, and verification indicators.

## 6. How do helpers get paid?
Helpers receive payout through the platform after completed bookings and according to payout rules.

## 7. Can I use the platform if I live alone or feel anxious before appointments?
Yes. The platform is designed for practical and emotional non-medical support around these moments.

## 8. Can international students or expats join as helpers?
Yes, if they meet platform requirements and complete profile and verification steps.

## 9. What if something goes wrong?
You can report issues through the platform. The platform includes moderation and review processes.

## 10. Do I have to pay outside the app?
No. Payments should stay inside the platform for a safer experience.
