# Startup Foundation v1

## Product Name
[Temporary Working Name]

## One-Sentence Definition
A trust-first two-sided marketplace that connects people who need non-medical support before or after medical processes with verified helpers who want flexible side income.

## Product Category
This product is a:
- two-sided marketplace
- trust-and-safety platform
- booking and coordination platform
- non-medical support service

This product is not:
- a telemedicine platform
- a hospital system
- a nursing service
- a medical transport company
- an emergency service
- a diagnosis or treatment platform

## Problem Statement
Many people need human support around medical appointments or procedures, but do not always have family, friends, or trusted contacts available. This need can arise for many reasons:
- they live alone
- they are elderly
- they are anxious before an appointment
- they are recovering after a minor procedure
- they are new to the city or country
- they face language or bureaucracy difficulties
- they want support getting to or from an appointment safely and calmly

At the same time, many students, internationals, part-time workers, and flexible workers want meaningful side income opportunities.

There is currently no simple, trust-first, non-medical marketplace specifically focused on matching these two groups in a structured way.

## Core Value Proposition

### For Clients
Book trusted non-medical support for moments before or after a medical appointment or procedure.

### For Helpers
Earn flexible side income by providing structured, meaningful, non-medical support.

## Why This Product Should Exist
This product addresses a real gap between medical care and everyday human support. Many people do not need a doctor or a nurse in these moments. They need:
- someone to accompany them
- someone to wait with them
- someone to help them get home
- someone to assist with simple non-medical practical tasks
- someone to reduce stress and isolation

The platform creates a formal and trusted way to organize this support.

## Why Now
Several conditions make this product timely:
- large metropolitan areas have many people living alone
- Berlin has a strong base of students, internationals, and expats
- more people are comfortable using platforms to find trusted services
- flexible side-income work is increasingly accepted
- people increasingly value convenience, safety, and human support
- urban life often weakens family-based support systems

## Target Market

### Launch City
Berlin only

### Launch Audience
Primary client segments:
- internationals

- people who need trusted support around appointments

Primary helper segments:
- students
- internationals

- multilingual people
- people comfortable with care-adjacent, non-medical support

## Core Marketplace Logic

### Client Side
A client can:
- create a support request
- browse helpers
- compare profiles and trust markers
- choose a helper
- confirm booking
- chat before the booking
- receive support
- review the helper afterward

### Helper Side
A helper can:
- sign up and build a profile
- submit verification information
- set availability
- browse open support requests
- accept or apply to requests
- chat with the client
- complete the booking
- receive payout
- build trust through ratings and completed jobs

## Trust Positioning
Trust is not an extra feature. Trust is the product foundation.

The platform must communicate:
- clear service boundaries
- visible verification
- secure in-platform booking and payment
- reporting and moderation tools
- strong safety and prohibited-task rules

## Launch Principle
The first launch version will be narrow, local, and disciplined:
- Berlin only
- non-medical support only
- no emergency use cases
- no clinical services
- strong trust-first product experience
