# Pitch Deck Map v1
## Purpose
To explain the business simply enough that someone understands the mechanics before they admire the vision.

## Slide Order (14 Slides Maximum)
1. Title / One-line pitch
2. Problem
3. Why now
4. Solution
5. Product flow
6. Marketplace model
7. Trust and safety design
8. Target market / launch wedge
9. Early traction / validation
10. Go-to-market
11. Defensibility / moat
12. Expansion path
13. Team / founder
14. Ask / next step
