# Sprint Overview — 4 Weeks v1

## Purpose
Move from planning into serious product construction without losing focus.

## Weekly Themes
- **Week 1:** Auth, roles, onboarding backbone (The Identity Layer).
- **Week 2:** Profiles, requests, and helper discovery (The Marketplace Layer).
- **Week 3:** Booking state logic, messaging, and admin foundations (The Transaction Layer).
- **Week 4:** Payments structure, trust/safety flow, and beta-readiness (The Protection Layer).

## Goal After 4 Weeks
A stable, "Steel Thread" executable product. Not a full launch, but a system that can handle a complete user journey.
