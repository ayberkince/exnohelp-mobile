# Cancellation Policy v1

## Purpose
This document defines how cancellations work for clients and helpers.

The cancellation policy must be:
- fair
- understandable
- enforceable
- supportable

---

## 1. Core Principle
Cancellations are allowed, but the rules must protect:
- client trust
- helper time
- platform integrity

---

## 2. Client Cancellation
### Client may cancel:
- before booking begins
- subject to cancellation timing rules

### Suggested logic
- early cancellation: full refund possible
- late cancellation: partial refund or reduced refund possible
- no-show: refund may be denied depending on policy

---

## 3. Helper Cancellation
### Helper may cancel:
- before booking begins
- but cancellations should be tracked carefully

### Rules
- repeated helper cancellations damage marketplace trust
- helper cancellation may affect helper reliability metrics
- admin review may be triggered for repeated cancellations

---

## 4. Platform / Admin Cancellation
The platform may cancel or intervene if:
- safety issue is identified
- payment issue occurs
- fraud issue appears
- booking is invalid or violates platform boundaries

---

## 5. Cancellation Statuses
Bookings may enter:
- canceled_by_client
- canceled_by_helper
- canceled_by_platform

---

## 6. Visibility Rule
Before payment, the user must see:
- basic cancellation policy summary
- where to find full policy
