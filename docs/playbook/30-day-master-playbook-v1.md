# 🚀 The Master Startup Playbook v1

## 1. The Core Identity (Days 1-5)
- **The Concept:** A trust-first marketplace for non-medical support around appointments.
- **The Engine:** Next.js App Router, Prisma, PostgreSQL, Stripe, NextAuth.
- **The Moat:** Trust, clear service boundaries, local density (Berlin first).
- **The Golden Rule:** We do not provide clinical care. We provide practical and emotional support.

## 2. The Build Sprints (Days 6-22)
- **Week 1 (Identity):** Auth, Roles (Client vs Helper), Onboarding flows.
- **Week 2 (Marketplace):** Support Requests, Discovery, Matching logic.
- **Week 3 (Transaction):** Bookings, Chat, State transitions (Pending -> In Progress -> Completed).
- **Week 4 (Finance & Trust):** Pricing math, Payment models, Report handling, Helper verification.

## 3. The Beta Operations (Days 23-26)
- **Readiness:** Do not launch until reports and verifications can be handled manually.
- **The Cohort:** First 10 Helpers, First 10 Clients. Hand-picked, staggered invites.
- **Founder Rhythm:** - Morning: Check logs, reports, active bookings.
  - Midday: Unblock users, review verifications.
  - Evening: Log feedback, prioritize tomorrow's fixes.
- **The Dashboard:** One single source of truth for Liquidity (Supply vs Demand) and Trust (Open Reports).

## 4. The Narrative & Outreach (Days 27-29)
- **The Pitch:** We sit between clinical healthcare and informal support. 
- **The Ask:** Stage-appropriate (beta users, local partners, specific advisors).
- **The Outreach:** Human, highly specific, permission-based. No spam. 

## 5. The Expansion Gate (Day 25)
- **Expand when:** Core flows succeed, trust is stable, local liquidity exists.
- **Hold when:** Manual intervention is high, but users are learning.
- **Repair when:** Core booking loop breaks, trust boundaries are violated.
