# Booking Reminders v1

## Purpose
This document defines reminder logic for bookings.

The goal is to reduce:
- no-shows
- forgotten bookings
- uncertainty
- support load

---

## Reminder Types

### Confirmation Reminder
Sent immediately after booking is confirmed.

### Pre-Booking Reminder
Sent before the booking to both client and helper.

Recommended timing:
- 24 hours before if booking is scheduled far enough ahead
- 2 hours before on same-day or near-term cases if needed later

### Day-Of Reminder
A short reminder on the day of booking.

### Follow-Up Reminder
Sent after completion to:
- request review
- confirm completion
- support repeat use

---

## Reminder Content Principles
Every reminder should clearly show:
- who
- what
- when
- where
- next step

---

## Trust Rule
Reminder messages should reinforce:
- in-app chat
- in-platform coordination
- non-medical support boundaries
