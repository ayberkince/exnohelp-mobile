# Transactional Email Flows v1

## Purpose
This document defines all critical email flows in v1.

Transactional emails are used when users need durable, external confirmation of important events.

---

## 1. Account Emails
- welcome email
- email verification email
- password reset email
- account review / restriction email
- account restored email

---

## 2. Onboarding Emails
### Client
- complete your profile
- add your emergency contact
- create your first support request

### Helper
- complete your profile
- finish verification
- start browsing open requests

---

## 3. Booking Emails
- booking confirmed
- booking canceled
- booking reminder
- booking completed
- review request after booking

---

## 4. Payment Emails
- payment successful
- payment failed
- refund update
- payout update

---

## 5. Trust and Safety Emails
- report confirmation if appropriate
- account warning if appropriate
- account restriction or suspension notice

---

## 6. v1 Email Rule
Email should be used for:
- durable confirmation
- important account state changes
- reminders that matter even when user is not inside the app
